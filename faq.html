Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our property care services.

What areas do you service?

We currently serve the entire metro area including surrounding suburbs. Please contact us with your address and we'll confirm if we can service your property.

How often should I have my lawn mowed?

During peak growing season (spring/early summer), most lawns benefit from weekly mowing. In slower growth periods, bi-weekly may be sufficient. We'll assess your specific lawn and recommend the ideal schedule.

Do you provide your own equipment?

Yes, we bring all necessary professional-grade equipment for each service. This includes mowers, trimmers, blowers, and any other tools needed to complete the job to our high standards.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, checks, and cash. For ongoing services, we also offer convenient automatic payment options.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. GreenEdge Property Care is fully licensed, bonded, and insured for your protection. We can provide proof of insurance upon request.

What if I need to reschedule a service?

We understand schedules change. Just give us at least 24 hours notice and we'll happily reschedule your service at no additional charge.

Still have questions?

We're happy to answer any other questions you may have about our services.
