Our Story

Founded in 2015, GreenEdge Property Care began as a small local business with a passion for transforming outdoor spaces. What started as a one-person operation has grown into a dedicated team serving our community with pride.

We believe that great outdoor spaces enhance quality of life, and we're committed to providing reliable, high-quality service that makes property maintenance effortless for our clients.
